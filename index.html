The Sinner's Prayer

"Dear Heavenly Father,

I know that I am a sinner and I need Your forgiveness. I believe that Jesus Christ died on the cross for my sins and rose again from the dead. I repent of my sins and ask You to forgive me.

Right now, I invite Jesus Christ to come into my heart and life as my personal Lord and Savior. I surrender my life to You and ask You to take control.

Thank You for loving me, forgiving me, and giving me eternal life. Help me to live for You from this day forward.

In Jesus' precious name I pray, Amen."

Prayer for Baptism in the Holy Spirit

"Heavenly Father,

I thank You for Your Son Jesus and for salvation. I now come to You asking for the baptism in the Holy Spirit. Jesus, You said that You would baptize us with the Holy Spirit and fire.

I believe Your Word that says the promise is for me. Fill me now with Your Holy Spirit. I receive this gift by faith. Give me the ability to speak in tongues as the Spirit gives me utterance.

Holy Spirit, I welcome You. Take control of my life. Use me for Your glory. Empower me to be a witness for Jesus Christ.

Thank You for filling me with Your Spirit. In Jesus' mighty name, Amen."
