1 The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing.
2 He makes me lie down in green pastures, he leads me beside quiet waters,
3 he refreshes my soul. He guides me along the right paths for his name's sake.
4 Even though I walk through the darkest valley, I will fear no evil, for you are with me; your rod and your staff, they comfort me.
5 You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies. You anoint my head with oil; my cup overflows.
6 Surely your goodness and love will follow me all the days of my life, and I will dwell in the house of the Lord forever.

Reflection

In this beloved psalm, we find comfort in knowing that God is our caring shepherd who provides, protects, and guides us through every season of life. His presence transforms our fears into faith, our valleys into victories, and our earthly journey into an eternal hope.
